About Me.
Hello! I am a 21 year old design student at Parsons Paris. Originally from New York City, and with over 7 years of diversified art school experience, I am a creator driven by my passion to enable accessibility and reach to the masses through my design work. My interests are primarily based in motion graphics design, editorial design, and UI/UX design. I am well versed in After Effects, InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Lightroom, HTML/CSS (JS) and have a base in Blender.
